Who We Are

India ranks second in farm output but has failed to eliminate the financial crises of farmers. AgriRetail.com is here to bridge this gap by providing farmers with assured contract farming opportunities and guaranteed pricing. We are revolutionizing the agricultural market by connecting farmers directly with retailers, offering transparency, better pricing, and crop insurance. Our platform empowers farmers by eliminating middlemen, securing payments, and providing professional support throughout the process.

What We Offer

Guaranteed Pricing

We offer prices that are better than the government-mandated minimum selling price, ensuring profitability for farmers.

Crop Insurance

Frequently Asked Questions

AgriRetail uses a transparent pricing mechanism and market analysis to offer farmers competitive rates above the minimum selling price.

Farmers can access the contract farming options by logging into their account on the portal and navigating to the "Make a Contract" section. Here, they can view available contracts and secure deals with retailers directly.

Crop insurance is optional but highly recommended for all farmers to protect their investments and ensure coverage in case of adverse weather conditions or other calamities.

The Quick Sell feature allows farmers to sell their products instantly toretailers who are facing stock shortages during emergencies, such as floods. Farmers can view the stock requirements from retailers in the portal and contact them directly to negotiate the sale of their produce. This feature is only activated during natural calamities and ensures timely transactions.
